RESIDENTIAL RENTAL AGREEMENT CONTRACT

This rental agreement is entered into between:

TENANT: John Smith, residing at 456 Oak Street, City, State 12345
LANDLORD: ABC Properties LLC, a limited liability company, located at 789 Business Avenue, City, State 12345

PROPERTY DESCRIPTION:
The rental property is located at 123 Main Street, Apartment 4B, City, State 12345. The property is a two-bedroom, one-bathroom apartment with approximately 900 square feet of living space.

RENTAL TERMS:
1. Monthly Rent: The tenant agrees to pay a monthly rental amount of $1,500.00 (One Thousand Five Hundred Dollars) due on the 1st day of each month.

2. Security Deposit: The tenant shall provide a security deposit of $1,500.00 prior to occupancy. This deposit will be returned upon termination of the lease, subject to deductions for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

3. Lease Duration: This agreement begins on January 1, 2024, and ends on December 31, 2024, unless terminated earlier according to the terms herein.

TENANT OBLIGATIONS:
- The tenant John Smith is responsible for timely payment of monthly rent
- The tenant must maintain the rental property in good condition
- The tenant agrees to pay all utilities including electricity, gas, water, and internet
- The tenant shall not sublease the property without written consent from the landlord
- The tenant must provide 30 days written notice before terminating the lease

LANDLORD OBLIGATIONS:
- ABC Properties LLC is responsible for major structural repairs and maintenance
- The landlord must ensure the property meets all local housing codes and safety requirements
- The landlord agrees to provide 24-hour notice before entering the property for non-emergency purposes
- The landlord is responsible for property taxes and building insurance

FINANCIAL OBLIGATIONS:
- Monthly rent payment is due on the 1st of each month without exception
- Late payment fee of $50.00 applies for rent received after the 5th of the month
- The tenant is liable for any damages beyond normal wear and tear, to be deducted from security deposit
- Upon lease termination, security deposit will be returned within 30 days, less any applicable deductions

TERMINATION CONDITIONS:
- Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice
- The landlord may terminate immediately for non-payment of rent or violation of lease terms
- The tenant may terminate immediately if the property becomes uninhabitable
- Upon termination, all financial obligations must be settled before the tenant vacates
