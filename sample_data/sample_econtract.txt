SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [DATE] between Service Provider ("Provider") and Client Company ("Client").

1. SERVICES
The Provider agrees to provide consulting services including but not limited to:
- Software development and maintenance
- Technical support and training
- System integration services
- Quality assurance testing

2. PAYMENT TERMS
The Client agrees to pay the Provider according to the following terms:
- Hourly rate: $150 per hour
- Payment due within 30 days of invoice
- Late payment penalty: 2% per month
- Travel expenses reimbursed at cost

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information including:
- Technical specifications and documentation
- Business processes and strategies
- Customer data and contact information
- Financial information and pricing

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All intellectual property created during the engagement shall be:
- Owned by the Client upon full payment
- Licensed to Provider for portfolio purposes
- Protected under applicable copyright laws
- Subject to non-disclosure agreements

5. TERMINATION
This Agreement may be terminated by either party with:
- 30 days written notice
- Immediate termination for breach of contract
- Completion of all outstanding obligations
- Return of confidential materials

6. LIABILITY AND WARRANTIES
The Provider warrants that services will be performed with professional competence.
Liability is limited to the amount paid under this Agreement.
No warranty is provided for third-party software or systems.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [STATE/COUNTRY].
Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.
